A Brief History

Eastern Balti House has been serving the Monaghan community for thirteen years, bringing the authentic flavours of Balti cuisine to your table. Our journey began with a passion for sharing delicious, traditional dishes and creating a warm, inviting atmosphere for everyone.

Which all began when Taus, one of the co-founders of this special business, initially came from Bangladesh to Dublin, Ireland, for the learning experience in the city centre where one of the restaurants he got a job in was called Eastern Balti House.

His elder cousin brother — another co-founder from Bangladesh — who was also in Dublin at the time in 2001, granted Taus a work permit.

Together with Taus and his two cousin brothers, they established the new Eastern Balti House in Monaghan in 2003, making sure the criterion for an Indian-style catering service to be set up in Monaghan, Ireland, was met.
